"Fairy tales are more than true: not because they tell us that dragons exist, but because they tell us that dragons can be beaten." Neil Gaiman.

I only started reading about 10 years ago, the first book I read was Secrets of the Millionaire Mind by T. Harv Eker, and it was a great one to start of, short, easy to read and enjoyable, from there I soon was reading Napoleon Hill's The Law of Success.

From self-development to romance, I love reading any good book, amongst my favorite books ever are The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho, The Book Thief by Markus Zusak, The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen Covey, Secrets of the Millionaire Mind by T. Harv, Alice Through the Looking-Glass by Lewis Carroll, just few of many favorites.

What I love most about reading is that I can travel to another worlds without leaving my chair.
